Leaving and Joining Maryland

People and States are allowed to join and leave Maryland at their own volition, but you can only leave join two times. after you join the second time, if you leave, you are not allowed to rejoin

To join as a state, you must ask the President and if they agree you will be admitted into the union.

If you wish to join as a person, ask the governer of which state you want to join and if they are accepted you become a citizen.
